CODI_BFT is a blufortracker.

When you open the map, you can see every unit of your own side in realtime.
When zooming out, units are grouped if they are close together.
When you zoom in, they get separated again.
Group leaders have a larger symbol and have the callsign attached.

ACE:
Only units who have the item CODI_BFT_Tablet can track other units.
Only units who have CODI_BFT_Tablet can be tracked.
It is not possible to track enemy units if you collect an enemytracker.
